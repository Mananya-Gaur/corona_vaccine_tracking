COVID VACCINE

After the aproval of drugs contoller general of india (DCGI),they have introduced two types of vaccine.
1. BHARAT BIOTECH'S COVAXIN

2. SERUM INSTITUTE OF INDIA'S COVISHIELD

After that Doctor Rahul Pandit,director-critical care,Fortis hospital Mumbai and member of Maharashtra's covid 19 tasks force has also considered and declared the vaccine safe for public use.

DCGI has given approval on the basis of recomendation submitted by covid 19 subject expert comittee(SEC) of central drugs standard control organisation(CDSCO).

COVISHIELD:
Covishield or AZD-1222 is originally developed by oxward university Astrazeneca and marketed as "covishield" by Pune based vaccine centre sereum institute of india. Global trial pegged oxford covishield vaccine to be at least 70.42% effective.

COVAXIN:

Covaxin is india's first fully developed and produced covid 19 vaccine made by Hyderabad based Bharat biotech.

Covid vaccine was launched on 16th January 2021











India vaccinates the highest number of persons on day first of nation wide covid 19.

As per reports both the covaxin and covishield can be stored at normal refrigeration condition and require -70 degree temperature.
